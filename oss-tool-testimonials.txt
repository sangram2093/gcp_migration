Executive summary
We reduced the total vulnerability count by almost half in two weeks using the dbSAIcle plugin and a structured workflow.

Jan 20, 2026: 1,947 vulnerabilities

What enabled the reduction (non‑technical explanation)

Focused on what matters most

Guided fixes and verification
The plugin suggested safe updates and verified results after changes, preventing rework.

Continuous tracking
We consistently tracked progress to keep the team aligned and highlight improvements.
